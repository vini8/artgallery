What is Salesforce Marketing Cloud?

What if you get a tool to automate various repetitive tasks that go into while setting up and running a successful marketing campaign?

With Salesforce Marketing Cloud, you get the power to put your business routine task on autopilot. In simple words, Salesforce Marketing Cloud is a bridge that connects your customers to a business. A full-fledged, sophisticated, and high featured Marketing Automation software possessing the power to automate monotonous marketing work in diverse dimensions.

Email Studio

This is an excellent product that enables marketers to reach their target customers effectively. The product creates advanced emails with data from various departments. Journey Builder

Journey Builder can be an incredible tool for campaign planning. It benefits marketers by allowing them to design and automate campaigns at the same time. The astonishing part is that marketers can guide their customers throughout their relationship with a brand. With Journey Builder, a marketer can strengthen their customer experience in a variety of ways, right from traditional methods to modern digital touchpoints such as emails, smartphones, and so on.

Advertising studio

Advertising Studio can be extremely beneficial to marketers when it comes to activating important data of key customers in order to improve online advertisements and enable a superior one-on-one customer experience. Interaction Studio

Mobile Studio

Mobile Studio is currently a solid product for personalizing mobile marketing. It facilitates marketers to reach out to customers without being limited by a device.
