Drivers have the option to accept or reject orders based on their availability and proximity.
  • Real-time Order TrackingOnce a driver accepts an order, they can track the delivery route and receive turn-by-turn directions to the pickup and drop-off locations. Real-time tracking helps drivers efficiently navigate and deliver orders on time.
  • In-app NavigationThe app integrates with GPS navigation systems to provide drivers with optimized routes, live traffic updates, and the estimated time of arrival (ETA). This feature assists drivers in reaching their destinations quickly and efficiently.
  • Order Status UpdatesDrivers can update the order status in real time within the app. They can indicate when they have arrived at the restaurant for pickup when they are en route to the delivery location, and when the order has been successfully delivered. These status updates keep both the restaurant and the customer.
  • Communication with Customers and RestaurantsThe app provides a communication channel for drivers to contact customers or restaurants if needed. Drivers can clarify delivery instructions, confirm pickup details, or resolve any issues that may arise during the delivery process.
  • Earnings and Payout ManagementThe app provides drivers with a clear overview of their earnings. They can track their completed deliveries, view detailed earnings reports, and request payouts. This feature ensures transparency and facilitates timely payments.
  • Ratings and FeedbackAfter each delivery, drivers can rate customers and provide feedback on their experience. This feedback system allows for continuous improvement and helps maintain a high level of service quality.
  • Availability ManagementDrivers can set their availability status within the app, indicating when they are ready to accept delivery requests. This feature allows drivers to control their working hours and manage their schedules effectively.
  • Support and AssistanceThe app offers support features for drivers, including access to customer support, help articles, and FAQs. Drivers can seek assistance for any technical issues or inquiries they may have.
