The Flexible Engagement Models we follow

We support a diverse set of engagement models that provide greater flexibility to our customers.

Dedicated Team Model

Under this Model, you hire a dedicated team of app developers in India who work solely on your project. A project manager oversees the team, and you have direct contact with the developers. This model is ideal for projects with a long timeline and a high level of complexity. You have complete control over the project and can make changes as needed. In the long run, the dedicated team approach is also the most cost-effective strategy.

Time and Material Model

In this model, you pay for the developers' time and resources spent on your project. When the requirements are not well specified or the project is expected to evolve over time, this paradigm works best. You just pay for the resources that you use, and you can scale your team as needed. This paradigm is suited for tasks with a high level of uncertainty and that necessitate extensive investigation and experimentation.

Fixed-Price Model

In this model, you pay a set fee for the entire project. The developers provide a thorough estimate of the time and resources needed to execute the project, and you pay a fixed price for those resources. This paradigm works best when the requirements are well-defined and the project scope is clear. The fixed-price approach is excellent for minor projects or those with a set deadline.

Staff Augmentation Model

Individual developers are hired to supplement your existing in-house development staff under this arrangement. This model is ideal for companies that already have a development team but require additional resources to meet their requirements. You may quickly scale your team to meet your needs and only pay for the resources you utilize. This model is also appropriate for companies with a well-defined development process and project scope.

The best engagement model for you will be determined by your unique demands and requirements. When determining which model is best for your company, consider your project objectives, timeline, and budget.

How Much Does it cost to hire a Mobile app developer in India?

The cost of hiring mobile app developers in Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, and Gurgaon India varies depending on a number of criteria, including the developer's experience, skill set, region, and project type. Hiring a mobile app developer in India might cost between $15 and $100 per hour on average.

A junior developer with 1-3 years of experience, for example, may charge $15 to $30 per hour, but a senior developer with 5+ years of expertise may charge $50 to $100 per hour.

Furthermore, the developer's location can have an impact on the cost. Developers in major cities such as Bangalore, Mumbai, and Delhi typically charge higher fees but with best quality than those in smaller towns.

Finally, the complexity and duration of the project are important factors in deciding the cost. A simple mobile app with limited functionality can cost $5,000 to $15,000, whereas a complicated app with extensive features might cost $50,000 or more.

Keep in mind that these rates may change depending on market demand and competition for developers with specific skill sets. Furthermore, outsourcing companies frequently charge a premium for their services, which can dramatically increase the overall cost.

It is also worth noting that these hourly rates are only estimates and may not accurately reflect the total cost of a project. Other considerations that can influence the cost of hiring a mobile app developer in India include the scope of the project, the completion deadline, and any additional resources or tools that may be required.
