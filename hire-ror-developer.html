Fixed Price Model

A fixed price model is appropriate for you if you are an entrepreneur with a small- to medium-sized project that has precise and well-defined needs. In this model, you are required to pay a predetermined sum or fixed price for the finished output.

The fixed price model allows you to explicitly specify your project's needs, specs, and schedules before the start of development, which reduces your risk. A set price approach has the advantage of requiring the least amount of management on your part.

We will take care of every facet of the development process, project management, quality analysis, and assurance. When it comes to reducing costs and creating an app on a budget, this model is regarded as one of the most successful ones. Even so, because of the project's defined timeframe, it must be finished within the allotted time frame.

Dedicated Team Model

If you are an entrepreneur who wants to develop a mobile application for your firm but doesn't have enough time to speak with each team member, such as a developer, designer, quality analyst, and web developer, then the dedicated team model is for you.

Under this approach, you can employ a full team, including ROR developers, a backend web developer, a UI/UX designer, a quality analyst, and a project manager to oversee the entire team.
