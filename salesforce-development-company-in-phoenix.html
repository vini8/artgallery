Tech stack we include in Salesforce development

In our Salesforce development services, we incorporate a robust and comprehensive tech stack that enhances the capabilities and flexibility of Salesforce. Our tech stack encompasses various technologies and tools that enable us to deliver high-quality and customized solutions. Here are some of the key components of our tech stack for Salesforce development:

Salesforce Platform

At the core of our tech stack is the Salesforce Platform itself, which provides a powerful foundation for developing scalable and secure applications. We leverage the declarative features of the platform, such as Process Builder, Flow Builder, and Lightning App Builder, to configure and customize Salesforce solutions without the need for extensive coding.

Apex

Apex is the programming language used exclusively for Salesforce development. It allows us to build custom logic and extend the functionality of Salesforce. With Apex, we develop triggers, controllers, and batch processes to automate complex business processes, integrate with external systems, and enhance the overall performance of your Salesforce implementation.

Visualforce

Visualforce is a markup language that enables the creation of custom user interfaces within Salesforce. It allows us to design and develop pixel-perfect pages, forms, and components to provide a highly tailored and intuitive user experience. We utilize Visualforce to build custom layouts, dashboards, and reports that meet your specific business requirements.

Lightning Web Components (LWC)

Lightning Web Components is a modern framework for building responsive and performant web applications on the Salesforce Platform. With LWC, we develop dynamic and interactive user interfaces that offer a seamless user experience across devices. LWC enables us to leverage modern web development practices and technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to deliver highly engaging and feature-rich interfaces.

Salesforce APIs

Salesforce offers a wide range of APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) that allow seamless integration with external systems and data sources. We leverage Salesforce APIs, such as REST API, SOAP API, and Bulk API, to enable bi-directional data exchange, synchronize data with other platforms, and integrate Salesforce with third-party applications and services.

Heroku

Heroku is a cloud platform that enables us to build, deploy, and manage applications outside of the Salesforce Platform. We leverage Heroku for building custom web applications, microservices, and APIs that integrate with Salesforce. This allows us to extend the functionality of Salesforce, develop scalable solutions, and accommodate specific business needs that go beyond the capabilities of the Salesforce Platform alone.

JavaScript Frameworks

We utilize popular JavaScript frameworks like React, Angular, and Vue.js to develop highly interactive and dynamic user interfaces. These frameworks enable us to create rich client-side experiences, handle complex data manipulation, and provide seamless integration with Salesforce data.
