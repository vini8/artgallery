Our Salesforce Data Migration Specialisation

Planning Migration

Our Salesforce data migration consultants assess the requirement, perform a feasibility check on data transfer, evaluate the data source and destination, create a roadmap, and develop a solid plan to transfer the data. We choose the right tool, method, and approach to move data from on-premise and cloud systems based on specific business requirements.

Data Standardization

Business functions get jammed when the data is gathered from various sources, and poor quality data can derail the entire process. We as a top Salesforce Migration Service Company standardize and optimize the data at maximum consistency and accuracy. We perform data cleansing and reduplication procedures as part of data standardization to remove useless records.

Data Mapping

When all objects, records, and information are correctly mapped then only the data migration process turns out to be successful. We map the data from various sources to the appropriate fields. Our data migration solutions aim to support various business operations and data analytics with the transformed data, rather than just field mapping.

Data Migration

We use the best ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) tools to migrate legacy and Salesforce to Salesforce data and upgrade native products. Our data migration teams move data quickly without disrupting existing workflows and processes by using an automated or custom Salesforce migration approach based on the data complexity and volume of the source data.

Quality Assurance

We are pro in a variety of testing tools and methodologies, and we carry out migrations as per your business requirements and problems. Our testing specialists make the best use of the developer console to validate the migrated records and test the deployed system by generating records with filter-based SOQL queries. Following the completion of the migration process, our QA teams evaluate the system using a variety of tests to ensure that all data is safely transferred in the correct formats.

Data Integrity

Our expertise in data migration includes the ability to maintain and protect our customers' data for consistency throughout the migration cycle. We validate the migrated instance with byte-check for privacy and security before enabling write permissions on the data in the new location, using sound integrity checks and validations.
