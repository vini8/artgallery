Methodology

One of the most common mistakes that a development company does is to ignore the previous backlogs. At Oak Tree Software, we follow the modular approach and breakdown the tasks in detail for grooming the backlogs to utilize them efficiently. Here’s a look at our approach to any project requirements-

Brainstorming

Any strategy starts with market analysis and competition research around the requirements. Based on the analysis and requirements gathering, we develop a POC that explains the required business model, technology innovation and identification of various modules.

Solutions Development Strategy

It starts with drafting and developing scalable roadmaps, selecting the right technology stack and developing a multiplatform strategy that assist in finding better business models.

Sprints

A proper sprint planning is what we exclusively believe in to prioritize and continuously improve the development process.
